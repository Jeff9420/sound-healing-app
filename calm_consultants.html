Guide your client’s employees throughout their journey

  • Calm Health delivers personalized recommendations based on responses to an in-app mental health screening
  • It guides individuals to additional support offered by your clients, serving as a gateway to employer-sponsored benefits and leveraging our high engagement rates to increase utilization
  • It promotes early intervention to avoid the escalation of stress, mild depressive symptoms, anxiousness, insomnia, and other challenges
  • It provides tools and resources to support employees during the time between recognizing a need for and accessing professional care
