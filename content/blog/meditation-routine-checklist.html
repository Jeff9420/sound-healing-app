Practice Toolkit

Daily Meditation Routine Checklist

Keep this checklist nearby to make consistency effortless—perfect for new practitioners and seasoned meditators who need a refresh.

1. Prepare Your Space

  • Choose a chair, cushion, or mat that keeps your spine naturally upright.
  • Dim bright lights; favor indirect lamps or candles.
  • Silence alarms, mute notifications, and let family/roommates know you are offline.

2. Set Your Intention

  • Pick one focus: calm, compassion, clarity, or energy.
  • State it silently three times, e.g., “Today I welcome calm with every breath.”
  • Open Sound Healing Space and select a playlist that matches your intention.

3. Dial In Your Audio

  • For morning sessions, try Morning Focus tracks around 60 BPM.
  • For evening wind-down, switch to Rain & nature collections.
  • Use the timer inside the player to automate your closing ritual.

4. Track Micro-Wins

  • Rate your calm level from 1-5 before and after the session.
  • Write one word that captures how you feel afterwards.
  • Snap a quick note in your phone or journal to reinforce the habit.

5. Review Weekly

  • Every Sunday, glance back at your notes to notice patterns.
  • Adjust the time of day or playlist based on energy levels.
  • Share one insight with a friend or community to stay accountable.
