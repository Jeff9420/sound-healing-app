Sleep Ritual

Create an Evening Reset Ritual

Detach from daytime stress and prime your mind for deep rest with this five-step routine.

1. Power Down

  • Close laptops and silence notifications 60 minutes before bed.
  • Switch lighting to warm temperature bulbs or candles.

2. Gentle Mobility

Spend five minutes with hip openers, spinal twists, and soft neck rolls while playing Coastal Flow.

3. Breath + Hypnosis

Select a Sleep Gateway hypnosis track, set a 20-minute timer, and follow a 4-7-8 breathing cycle.

4. Gratitude Journal

  • Write down three micro-wins from the day.
  • Note one thing you are releasing before sleep.

5. Drift Off

Switch to a low-volume rain mix and allow it to fade out with the Sound Healing Space sleep timer.

Save this ritual inside your notes app and explore more nighttime tools in the Sound Healing Space app.
