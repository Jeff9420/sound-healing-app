Facilitator Guide

How to Lead a Group Sound Bath

Design immersive sessions for clients, retreats, or corporate wellness teams using portable audio resources.

Setup Essentials

  • Arrive 30 minutes early to tune your space, lights, and temperature.
  • Lay down mats, bolsters, blankets, and eye masks in a circular pattern.
  • Place speakers or bowls equidistant from participants to balance the field.

Session Flow

  1. Welcome & consent check-in (5 min)
  2. Breath priming with Meditation Music (10 min)
  3. Layer bowls and chimes with Sound Healing Space bowls (30 min)
  4. Guided integration + journaling (10 min)
  5. Closing circle + resource share (5 min)

Aftercare Tips

  • Offer hydration and light snacks post-session.
  • Encourage 24 hours of gentle activity and minimal screen time.
  • Send a follow-up email with playlists and journaling prompts.
