What Makes Rain Audio Effective?

Rainfall produces broadband noise that masks unpredictable spikes from traffic, neighbors, or digital notifications. By keeping your brain in a steady sensory envelope, rain ambience promotes parasympathetic recovery and longer slow-wave sleep cycles.
