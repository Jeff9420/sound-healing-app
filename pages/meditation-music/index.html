Meditation Music FAQ

Do I need music to meditate?

No, but music can help beginners quiet the mind more easily. It provides a focal point and masks distracting environmental sounds, making it easier to maintain concentration.

How long should I meditate each day?

Start with 5-10 minutes daily. Research shows even brief sessions reduce stress and improve focus. Gradually increase to 20-30 minutes as your practice develops.

What's the best time to meditate?

Early morning sets a calm tone for the day, while evening meditation aids sleep. The best time is whenever you can practice consistently - habit matters more than timing.
