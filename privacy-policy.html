Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 8, 2025 Data Controller: SoundFlows Team

1. Overview

SoundFlows is committed to protecting your privacy while delivering an immersive sound healing experience. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store personal data when you use the SoundFlows web application.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and avatar when you register or sign in.
  • Playback activity: Favorite tracks, play history, custom mixes, and sleep timer choices.
  • Device data: Browser type, language settings, device type, and approximate region.
  • Analytics events: Aggregated usage metrics to help us improve personalization and performance.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide multilingual playback, focus mode, and personalized recommendations.
  • Sync favorites and history across your authenticated devices.
  • Measure app stability, accessibility, and feature adoption to prioritize improvements.
  • Send critical updates or security notifications when you opt in to email communication.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on a small set of privacy-aware partners. Each service only receives the minimum data required to deliver its function.

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore: Secure login, anonymous usage, and encrypted storage of account data.
  • Amplitude & Google Analytics 4: Aggregated product analytics and performance tracking.
  • Google Tag Manager: Streamlined management of measurement scripts.

5. Data Retention

  • Account and profile data: retained while the account remains active or until you request deletion.
  • Playback preferences and stats: retained for 24 months and purged if inactive.
  • Analytics events: stored in aggregated form for up to 25 months.

6. Your Rights

You can request to view, update, export, or delete your personal data at any time. Please contact us using the info below and we will respond within 30 days.

  • Access a copy of your stored data.
  • Correct inaccurate profile information.
  • Erase your account and associated history.
  • Withdraw consent for analytics or marketing communications.

7. Security Practices

We enforce TLS encryption, Firebase security rules, role-based access for administrators, and quarterly penetration reviews. Credentials are never stored in plain text.

8. Contact

Have a privacy question or need help exercising your rights?

Email the data protection team at privacy@soundflows.app
